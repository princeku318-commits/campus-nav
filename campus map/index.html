How it works

01
Open the map
Launch campus_map.html in any modern browser — no install, no login, no API key.
02
Enable location
Tap "My Location" to drop your GPS pin. Works on Android and iPhone over HTTP/HTTPS.
03
Pick a destination
Search or tap any building marker. Hit "Navigate here" to calculate the walking route.
04
Walk there
Follow the blue route line. Distance and estimated time shown in the status bar.
Visual flexibility

Three map styles

Switch between Dark mode for low-light use, Satellite for real aerial imagery of your campus, and Street view for classic OpenStreetMap detail. One tap, instant switch.
